Frequently Asked Questions

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Typically 6-8 sessions are recommended, spaced 4-6 weeks apart. Hair grows in cycles, so multiple sessions are needed to target all follicles at the right stage. After completing the full course, most patients achieve 80-90% permanent hair reduction with minimal maintenance.

Is Laser Hair Removal Painful?

expand_more
Discomfort is minimal - most patients describe it as a light snap or warmth on the skin. Our advanced laser systems include built-in cooling technology to maximise comfort throughout the session. Most patients find it far more comfortable than waxing.

Is It Safe For Dark Indian Skin Tones?

expand_more
Yes. Our trained specialists select the appropriate laser type and settings for your specific skin tone during your consultation. We safely and effectively treat all Indian skin tones, from fair to very deep, without risk of burns or pigmentation issues when done correctly.

Is There Any Downtime After The Session?

expand_more
No downtime. You can return to work or daily activities immediately. Mild redness or warmth in the treated area may occur for a few hours, which fades quickly. We advise avoiding sun exposure and applying SPF to treated areas as part of aftercare.

Can I Shave Between Sessions?

expand_more
Yes, shaving between sessions is perfectly fine and even recommended to keep the treatment area clean. However, avoid waxing, threading, or plucking between sessions as these remove the hair follicle that the laser needs to target.
