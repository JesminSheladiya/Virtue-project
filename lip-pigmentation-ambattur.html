Frequently Asked Questions

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Most patients see significant improvement in 2-3 sessions. However, the exact number depends on the depth of pigmentation and your lifestyle habits.

Is The Procedure Painful?

expand_more
We use advanced cooling technology and optional numbing creams. Most patients describe the sensation as a light tingling or flicking rubber band, but it is not painful.

Are The Results Permanent?

expand_more
The pigmentation removal is permanent, but maintenance is required if the root cause (like smoking or sun exposure) continues. Using SPF on your lips daily helps maintain results indefinitely.

Is It Safe For Sensitive Skin?

expand_more
Yes, our treatments are specifically designed for sensitive areas. Our dermatologists perform a patch test if needed to ensure zero adverse reactions.

What Is The Post-Care Like?

expand_more
It's simple: keep lips hydrated with a prescribed balm, avoid spicy foods for 24 hours, and strictly use lip SPF. You can return to work immediately.

Where Is Virtue Clinic Located?

expand_more
We are located in the heart of Ambattur, making premium aesthetic care accessible to local residents without the need for long travel.
