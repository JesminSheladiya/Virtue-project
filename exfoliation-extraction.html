Frequently Asked Questions

Is The Treatment Painful?

expand_more
The procedure is generally very comfortable. Medical-grade exfoliation involves no discomfort. Mild pressure may be felt during extraction of deeply clogged blackheads, which is brief and well-tolerated. Most patients find it significantly more comfortable than DIY extraction at home.

Is There Any Downtime After The Session?

expand_more
No downtime at all. You can resume daily activities immediately after your session. Mild redness may occur in the treated areas for a few hours and settles quickly. We advise avoiding heavy makeup for 12 hours and applying SPF to protect freshly cleared pores.

How Often Should I Get This Treatment?

expand_more
For mild one-time congestion, a single session delivers excellent results. For ongoing oily or combination skin prone to recurring blackheads, we recommend a session every 4-6 weeks. Maintenance sessions once every 1-2 months help keep skin consistently clear.

Is It Safe For Sensitive Skin?

expand_more
Yes. We customise exfoliation strength and extraction technique to your skin's sensitivity level. Patients with sensitive skin receive gentler formulations and extra care during extraction. Your specialist will assess your skin thoroughly before selecting the appropriate approach.

Can This Be Combined With Other Treatments?

expand_more
Yes. Exfoliation & Extraction pairs excellently with Hydra Facials, Glow Facials, or Mesotherapy for enhanced results. Your skin specialist will recommend the best combination based on your concerns and goals during your free consultation.
