Frequently Asked Questions

Is Body Contouring a Weight Loss Treatment?

expand_more
No. Body contouring is not a weight loss solution - it is a body shaping and fat reduction treatment. It targets stubborn, localised fat deposits in specific areas that are resistant to diet and exercise. It is most effective for individuals who are close to their ideal body weight but have specific areas of concern they want to refine and define.

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Typically 3-6 sessions are recommended, spaced 3-4 weeks apart. The number depends on the size of the treatment area, the amount of localised fat, and your desired outcome. Results develop gradually as your body processes and eliminates the targeted fat cells. Your specialist will advise your personalised plan at consultation.

Is There Any Downtime After The Treatment?

expand_more
Downtime is minimal or none. Temporary redness, mild swelling, or sensitivity in the treated area may occur for a few hours to a day. Most patients return to normal activities the same day. Your specialist will share simple aftercare instructions to maximise results and comfort.

When Will I See Results?

expand_more
Results appear gradually over several weeks as your body processes the treated fat cells naturally. Most patients begin noticing visible changes 4-8 weeks after their first session. Results continue to improve with each subsequent session. Full results are typically visible 8-12 weeks after the final session.

Are The Results Permanent?

expand_more
Results are long-lasting when maintained with a healthy lifestyle. The treated fat cells are permanently eliminated by your body. However, remaining fat cells in the area can still expand with weight gain. Maintaining a stable weight and healthy habits helps preserve results long-term. Maintenance sessions every 6-12 months are optional.
