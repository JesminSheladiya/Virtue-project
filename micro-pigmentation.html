Frequently Asked Questions

Does Micro Pigmentation Look Natural?

expand_more
Yes - when done by a trained specialist. Our practitioners use micro-dots that closely mimic natural hair follicles in size, shape, and spacing. Pigment shade is precisely matched to your natural hair colour. The result is indistinguishable from real hair density.

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Typically 2-3 sessions are recommended, spaced 2-4 weeks apart. The first session builds the base density, subsequent sessions refine, darken, and add texture. Results are visible immediately after Session 1 and progressively improved with each follow-up.

Is Micro Pigmentation Permanent?

expand_more
It is long-lasting - results typically last 3-5 years before gradual, natural fading occurs. Touch-up sessions every few years maintain density and colour. Unlike traditional tattoos, SMP pigments are designed to fade naturally and gracefully over time.

Is There Any Downtime After The Procedure?

expand_more
Minimal downtime. Mild redness and sensitivity in the scalp may occur for 24-48 hours after each session. Most patients resume normal daily activities the same day. We advise avoiding intense sun exposure, swimming, and heavy sweating for 5-7 days post-session.

Can It Be Combined With PRP Or Hair Transplant?

expand_more
Yes. Micro Pigmentation pairs excellently with PRP therapy and Mesotherapy for combined biological + cosmetic results. It also works superbly as a post-hair transplant density filler for areas between grafts. Your specialist will recommend the best combination at your consultation.
