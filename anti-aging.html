Frequently Asked Questions

At What Age Should I Start Anti-Aging Treatments?

expand_more
Preventive treatments can start as early as the late 20s or early 30s before visible signs appear. Early intervention helps slow collagen loss and maintain skin health. For those with visible fine lines, wrinkles, or laxity, treatment is highly effective at any age. Your dermatologist will recommend the most appropriate approach for your age and concern.

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Mild aging concerns typically respond in 3-4 sessions. Moderate signs of aging such as deeper lines and laxity require 4-6 sessions. Advanced concerns may need combination or maintenance treatments. Sessions are spaced based on skin response and treatment type. Your personalised plan will be discussed at your free consultation.

Will The Results Look Natural?

expand_more
Yes, our philosophy is gradual, natural rejuvenation. We focus on restoring what time has reduced like collagen, firmness, hydration, and glow. Results develop slowly over weeks, and most patients receive compliments without anyone being able to identify a specific treatment.

Is There Any Downtime?

expand_more
Downtime is minimal and varies by treatment. Most patients resume normal activities the same day. Mild redness or warmth may occur for a few hours after certain procedures. Your specialist will advise specific aftercare instructions to maximise results and minimise any post-treatment sensitivity.

Can Anti-Aging Treatments Be Combined?

expand_more
Yes, combination therapy often delivers the best results. Skin tightening (HIFU/RF/MNRF), collagen-boosting procedures, Mesotherapy, PRP, and rejuvenating facials can be combined in a personalised plan that addresses multiple aspects of aging simultaneously. Your dermatologist will design the most effective combination for your specific concerns.
