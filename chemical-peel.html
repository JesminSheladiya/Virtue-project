Frequently Asked Questions

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Typically 3-6 sessions are recommended, spaced 3-4 weeks apart to allow skin to renew between sessions. Mild concerns like dullness may show significant improvement in 3 sessions; deeper pigmentation or acne scars may need 5-6. Your dermatologist will advise a personalised plan during your free consultation.

Do Chemical Peels Hurt?

expand_more
Mild tingling or warmth is commonly felt during the session - but discomfort is minimal and brief. The sensation varies based on the peel strength selected for your skin. Your dermatologist will guide you through every step. Most patients find it very tolerable.

Is Chemical Peel Safe For Dark Indian Skin Tones?

expand_more
Yes, when performed by a trained dermatologist with the correct peel type and strength. Indian skin requires specific peel formulations to avoid post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. At Virtue, we customise every peel to your melanin level and skin sensitivity to ensure complete safety.

How Soon Will I See Results?

expand_more
Skin brightness improvement is often noticeable within 5-7 days of your first session as the old layer peels and fresh skin emerges. Pigmentation, acne marks, and texture improvements become progressively more visible with each subsequent session.

Can I Resume Normal Activities After A Peel?

expand_more
Yes. You can resume daily activities immediately. We advise applying SPF 50+ sunscreen daily, avoiding direct sun exposure for 5-7 days, and following the prescribed aftercare routine. Mild peeling or flaking is normal and settles within a few days.
