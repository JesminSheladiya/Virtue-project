Frequently Asked Questions

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Small tattoos typically require 3-5 sessions. Medium tattoos need 5-8 sessions. Large or multicolour tattoos may require 8-12 sessions or more. Sessions are spaced 4-6 weeks apart to allow pigment clearance. Your specialist will estimate the expected number of sessions at your free consultation based on tattoo size, ink colour, and depth.

Is Laser Tattoo Removal Painful?

expand_more
Mild discomfort is commonly described as a rubber band snapping against the skin. The sensation varies by tattoo location and skin sensitivity. Sessions typically last 15-45 minutes. Most patients find it very tolerable, especially for smaller tattoos. Your specialist will advise on comfort measures during your consultation.

Will The Tattoo Be Completely Removed?

expand_more
Most tattoos fade significantly - many achieve complete removal with the right number of sessions. Complete removal depends on ink type, colour, depth, age of the tattoo, and skin type. Black and dark inks respond best. Lighter colours like green, yellow, and blue may need additional sessions. Your specialist will advise realistic expectations at consultation.

Can All Tattoo Colours Be Removed?

expand_more
Black and dark navy inks respond most effectively to laser. Coloured inks like red, green, blue, yellow and more, can be targeted with specific laser wavelengths, though they may require more sessions. Your specialist will assess your tattoo colours and advise the most appropriate approach at your free consultation.

Is There Any Scarring After Treatment?

expand_more
When performed correctly by trained professionals on appropriately calibrated settings, the risk of scarring is minimal. Temporary redness, mild swelling, or blistering in the treated area is normal and resolves with proper aftercare. Following the post-treatment care instructions provided by our team is essential to prevent complications.
