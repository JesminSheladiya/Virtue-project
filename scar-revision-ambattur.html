Frequently Asked Questions

Can Scars Be Completely Removed?

expand_more
Complete removal is not always possible scar revision significantly reduces the visibility of scars and helps them blend more naturally with surrounding skin. Most patients achieve 50-80% or more improvement with the right combination approach. Your dermatologist will give you a realistic assessment of expected improvement at your free consultation.

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Mild scars typically respond in 3-4 sessions. Moderate scars generally require 4-6 sessions. Deep or complex scars may need combination therapy across more sessions. Sessions are spaced 3-4 weeks apart to allow collagen remodelling between visits. Your personalised plan will be discussed at consultation.

Is Scar Revision Painful?

expand_more
Discomfort is minimal and managed with topical anaesthetic cream applied before each session. Most patients find the procedure very comfortable throughout. Post-treatment, mild redness or sensitivity in the treated area resolves within a few days.

Can Scar Revision Be Done On All Skin Types?

expand_more
Yes, with proper assessment and appropriate technique selection. Indian skin requires carefully chosen parameters to avoid post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation or worsening of scar appearance. Our certified dermatologists assess your skin type and scar characteristics before selecting the safest, most effective approach for your specific case.

When Will I See Visible Results?

expand_more
Visible improvement appears gradually over multiple sessions as collagen remodels and the scar softens and blends. Most patients notice a meaningful change after 2-3 sessions. Full results from a complete course typically develop over 3-6 months post-treatment as collagen remodelling continues.
