Frequently Asked Questions

Can Acne Scars Be Completely Removed?

expand_more
Acne scars can be significantly reduced - in many cases by 60-80% or more with the right combination therapy. Complete removal depends on scar type, depth, and skin response. Your dermatologist will give you a realistic assessment and expected improvement percentage during your free consultation.

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Mild acne or post-acne marks typically respond in 3-4 sessions. Moderate acne scars require 4-6 sessions. Deep or pitted scars may need 6-8 sessions or combination therapy. Sessions are spaced 3-4 weeks apart to allow skin renewal between visits.

Is Acne Treatment Painful?

expand_more
Discomfort varies depending on the treatment method selected for your skin. Mild tingling or warmth is common. Advanced procedures like microneedling use topical numbing to minimise discomfort. Most patients find the procedures very well tolerated throughout the session.

Is It Safe For Sensitive Or Dark Indian Skin?

expand_more
Yes. Our certified dermatologists specialise in treating Indian skin tones. Treatment protocols are carefully calibrated to avoid post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation - a concern specific to melanin-rich skin. Every treatment decision is made with your skin's long-term health in mind.

How Soon Will I See Results?

expand_more
Most patients notice reduced inflammation, fewer active breakouts, and improved skin texture within the first 1-2 sessions. Scar improvement and pigmentation fading develop progressively over the full treatment course. Full results are typically seen 4-8 weeks after completing all sessions.
