Frequently Asked Questions

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Most patients see significant glow and hydration improvement after just 1 session. For lasting skin health and anti-aging benefits, we recommend a session every 3–4 weeks. Your skin specialist will guide you based on your specific skin type and goals.

Is The Facial Suitable For Sensitive Skin?

expand_more
Yes. Our gentle, medical-grade serums and procedures are safe for all skin types, including sensitive, reactive, and acne-prone skin. Our dermatologists customise the serum selection to ensure zero adverse reactions.

Is There Any Downtime?

expand_more
No downtime at all. You can return to work, apply light makeup, and carry on with your day immediately after the facial. Most patients walk out glowing and ready to go.

How Soon Will I See Results?

expand_more
Immediate hydration and glow are noticeable after your very first session. Skin looks refreshed, plump, and radiant straight away. With regular sessions, long-term improvements in texture, elasticity, and tone develop over 4–6 weeks.

Can Facials Be Combined With Other Treatments?

expand_more
Absolutely. Hydra and Glow Facials pair beautifully with chemical peels, mesotherapy, or acne scar correction for enhanced results. Your dermatologist will recommend the best combination for your skin concerns.
