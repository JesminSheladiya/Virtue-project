Frequently Asked Questions

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Most patients with moderate acne scars or skin texture concerns achieve significant improvement in 1-2 sessions. Those with deeper scars or more extensive concerns may benefit from 2-3 sessions. Each session is typically spaced 3-6 months apart to allow full collagen remodelling. Your dermatologist will advise the optimal number at your free consultation.

Is CO₂ Laser Treatment Painful?

expand_more
Topical anaesthetic cream is applied before the procedure to ensure your comfort throughout. Most patients experience mild warmth or a tingling sensation during treatment. Post-treatment, the skin feels similar to a moderate sunburn for a day or two, which is managed with the prescribed aftercare routine.

What Is The Downtime After CO₂ Laser?

expand_more
Initial healing takes 5-10 days during which the skin may appear red and peel as fresh skin emerges. Redness reduces over 1-2 weeks. Visible skin improvement becomes apparent at 4-6 weeks. Collagen remodelling and progressive improvement continue for up to 3-6 months post-treatment. Your specialist will provide a detailed healing timeline at consultation.

Is CO₂ Laser Safe For Indian Skin Tones?

expand_more
Yes, when calibrated correctly by a trained dermatologist. Indian skin requires carefully adjusted settings to achieve scar improvement without pigmentation changes. Your dermatologist will assess your skin type and customise the treatment for safe, effective results.

Can CO₂ Laser Be Combined With Other Treatments?

expand_more
Yes. CO₂ laser can be combined with PRP therapy for enhanced collagen stimulation and faster healing. It can also complement Mesotherapy, chemical peels, or MNRF for comprehensive skin rejuvenation. Your dermatologist will design the most effective combination plan for your specific goals.
