Frequently Asked Questions

What Is Face PRP Treatment?

expand_more
Face PRP utilizes your own blood plasma, which is rich in platelets, to encourage collagen production and tissue healing. It is a natural way to improve skin texture, reduce fine lines, and minimize acne scars.

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Mild texture issues typically respond in 2 to 3 sessions. Deeper acne scars or prominent fine lines require more sessions. Sessions are spaced based on your skin response. Your personalised plan will be discussed at your free consultation.

Is Face PRP Painful?

expand_more
Most treatments involve minimal discomfort. We apply a topical numbing cream before the procedure to ensure you are comfortable. You might feel a mild tingling, which is brief and very well tolerated. Your dermatologist will ensure a smooth process.

Is It Safe For Dark Indian Skin Tones?

expand_more
Yes, when performed by a trained dermatologist using the right protocol. Face PRP is highly safe because it uses your own plasma, eliminating the risk of allergic reactions. At Virtue, we specifically choose gentle methods appropriate for all Indian skin tones.

How Soon Will I See Results?

expand_more
Visible improvements in skin texture and tone take time and depend on the individual. Progressive improvement continues with each subsequent session. Full results from a complete course are typically visible a few weeks after your treatments as collagen builds up.
