Frequently Asked Questions

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Most patients see significant skin improvement in 3–6 sessions. Initial sessions are ideally spaced 2–4 weeks apart. Once skin health improves, maintenance sessions every 4–6 weeks sustain and deepen results. Your dermatologist will recommend a personalised plan during your free consultation.

Is Mesotherapy Painful?

expand_more
Discomfort is minimal. A topical numbing cream is applied before the session. Most patients describe the sensation as a very light tingling or micro-pinch. The procedure takes 30-45 minutes and is well-tolerated by all skin types

Is There Any Downtime After The Session?

expand_more
No downtime. You can return to work or daily activities immediately. Some patients experience mild redness for a few hours, which fades quickly. We recommend avoiding heavy makeup for 12 hours and following the prescribed aftercare routine for best results.

How Soon Will I See Results?

expand_more
Most patients notice improved hydration and a subtle glow immediately after their first session. With each subsequent session, skin becomes progressively brighter, plumper, and more even-toned. Full results from a complete course are typically visible within 6-8 weeks.

Is Mesotherapy Safe For Indian Skin?

expand_more
Yes. Mesotherapy is safe and highly effective for all Indian skin tones. Our dermatologists customise the Meso cocktail specifically for your skin concern, melanin type, and sensitivity level - ensuring maximum efficacy with minimal risk of irritation or pigmentation changes.
