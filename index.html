Honest Answers Before You Decide

Top questions from patients before starting treatment at Virtue Aesthetic Clinic.

Is the consultation really free?

expand_more
Yes — 100% free. Dr. Prakash or Dr. Venu Jayaram will personally assess your skin, diagnose your concern, and recommend a plan with no obligation and no hidden charges. We confirm your slot within minutes.

How many sessions will I need?

expand_more
It depends on your concern. Mild acne or pigmentation responds in 3–4 sessions. Complex concerns like deep scars or tattoo removal may need 5–8 sessions. Your dermatologist will give a clear, realistic estimate during your free consultation.

Is laser tattoo removal painful?

expand_more
Most patients describe it as a snapping sensation — mild and well-tolerated. We apply topical numbing cream before each session to keep you comfortable. The number of sessions depends on the tattoo's size, colour, age, and your skin tone.

Is it safe for Indian skin tones?

expand_more
Absolutely. Our MD Dermatologists specialise in treating Indian skin. Every protocol is calibrated to avoid post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation — a concern specific to melanin-rich skin. We never use a one-size-fits-all approach.

How soon will I see results?

expand_more
Most patients notice visible improvements within the first 1–3 sessions. Full results develop progressively over the treatment course, typically 4–8 weeks after completing sessions.
