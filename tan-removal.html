Frequently Asked Questions

Can Tan Be Removed Permanently?

expand_more
Tan can be significantly reduced and your natural skin tone restored. With consistent sun protection (SPF 50+ daily) after treatment, results are very long-lasting. Without proper sun protection, tanning can recur with continued UV exposure. Our team provides SPF guidance and aftercare advice to help you maintain your results.

How Many Sessions Will I Need?

expand_more
Mild or recent tan typically responds in 1-2 sessions. Moderate tan requires 3-4 sessions. Deep or long-term accumulated tan may need multiple sessions for progressive improvement. Sessions are spaced based on your skin's response. Your personalised plan will be discussed at your free consultation.

Is Tan Removal Painful?

expand_more
Most tan removal treatments involve minimal discomfort. Mild tingling or warmth may be felt during certain procedures, which is brief and very well tolerated. There is no pain with the gentler treatments. Your dermatologist will select the most comfortable approach for your skin type.

Is Tan Removal Safe For Dark Indian Skin Tones?

expand_more
Yes, when performed by a trained dermatologist using the right protocol. At Virtue, we specifically choose gentle, melanin-safe methods appropriate for all Indian skin tones. We avoid harsh bleaching agents that can cause damage on melanin-rich skin. Our goal is to restore your natural complexion - not to whiten or bleach.

How Soon Will I See Results?

expand_more
Visible brightening and improved skin tone are often noticeable after your very first session. Progressive improvement continues with each subsequent session. Full results from a complete course are typically visible 2-4 weeks after your last treatment as cell turnover completes.
